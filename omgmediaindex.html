Well we all know social media is for creating a community and then engaging them and leveraging for different purposes… But the challenge lies in, first generating any engagement and second, generating engagement for fulfilling your purpose!

So what businesses actually need is “Smarter Engagement” that deepens the relationship with its consumers for stirring up the emotions leading to meaning full interactions consistently beyond “15 minutes of fame” and expand the community with affinity audience.

Brand loyalty is so passé, “Identity Loyalty” is the way to go! When you create a connection with your audience so deep to make them believe in you, feel you, your brand becomes their expression leading to recommendations and evangelism.

If you have decided to #gosocial (only If that was a choice anymore…) it is a chance to transform your brand to be more humane, inclusive, narrative and most importantly to be the part of a community making your more approachable, “one of us” and power to make people listen!

Let’s admit the crowd and spam are on an increase and attention spans are at an all-time low on the internet and social media channels. Sensationalism for cheap “15 mins of fame” is ruining things further. With this social trend, a great deal of pressure is on “Quality Content”.

A good piece of social media content must be able to create a desire and a feeling of reward for your community to share your content or express themselves.

A right kind of content will address the need of belonging and self-validation to make your audience COMMENT | CRITIQUE | CONTRIBUTE

Think of social media as an extension, as an amplifier for your marketing efforts. Connect with your audience in real time and in person to get instant feedback on your marketing campaigns.

Respond to consumers and prospects, create a bond and have one-on-one conversation by addressing and recognizing them personally.

Create social graphs to identify influencers and prioritize them, create exclusive or preferential offers to strengthen the community for advocacy.

Social media isn’t a technical necessity for search engine optimization (SEO), but it surely boost the efforts as a pillar of strength. Social signals have a considerable say in search engine algorithms due to relevancy, currency and experience.

Social presence and conversations corresponds to the process of SEO by addressing the base of search engines quality parameters – “User Experience”.

Better engagement leads to relevant website traffic consistently and since the audience is already familiar or at least acquainted with you, the data consumption on website is meaningful and natural leading to improvement in bounce rates, time on site, interaction rate and more…

You can measure SUCCESS only when you know what it is to you. Social measurement should be aligned with the goal to have meaningful data. In general, there are two kinds of analytics in social media management, i) Ongoing analytics, ii) Campaign focused analytics.

Metrics and tools must be chosen and setup depending on your goals for appropriate data collection. A specific cost can be attributed to actions for measuring ROIs.

Social media platforms have immense options for measurement like demographics, interest levels, consumer behaviour, trends, insights on timelines, influencers and much more. Choose wisely!

It’s the Nirvana of social media goals. We all want to achieve it! It’s desired by one and all be it a B2B, B2C or a Personal Brand.

Since it is an ultimate goal you need to create a path and walk on it with discipline, commitment, and hard work. Take one step at a time.
